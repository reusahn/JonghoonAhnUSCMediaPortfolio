Gang Se-hwang AI Docent

2022 · Interactive Art · 경기도박물관-Gyeonggi Provincial Museum

Gang Se-hwang—renowned painter and mentor to Kim Hong-do—as an AI human. Recreated through real-time interaction, Gang Se-hwang serves as a docent at the Gyeonggi Provincial Museum, guiding visitors through historical narratives and offering photo-taking experiences. The installation reimagines cultural memory through artificial intelligence, allowing audiences to meet and interact with a digitally embodied scholar of the Joseon Dynasty in a hybrid time-space.
