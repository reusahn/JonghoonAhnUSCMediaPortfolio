Beads Wall

2025 · Media Installation · Calarts

Beads Wall is a media installation that transforms human motion into abstract compositions made of colorful beads, rendered in real time on a large digital wall.

The work visualizes the presence and gestures of passersby as shimmering pointillist silhouettes—where movement becomes material, and the human form dissolves into vibrant patterns.

A gel medium layered in front of the screen adds tactile dimensionality, blurring the boundary between virtual imagery and physical texture.
